What is lorem ipsum?

Show
Lorem Ipsum is dummy text used by designers and developers to fill space in a layout. It looks like normal text but has no real meaning. This helps people focus on how a page or design looks without being distracted by actual content.

Why do we use it?

Show
It is a long established fact that a reader will be distracted by the readable content of a page when looking at its layout. The point of using Lorem Ipsum is that it has a more-or-less normal distribution of letters, as opposed to using 'Content here, content here', making it look like readable English. Many desktop publishing packages and web page editors now use Lorem Ipsum as their default model text, and a search for 'lorem ipsum' will uncover many web sites still in their infancy. Various versions have evolved over the years, sometimes by accident, sometimes on purpose (injected humour and the like).

Where does it come from?

Show
The term Lorem Ipsum refers to a scrambled, non-sensical Latin text widely used by designers and publishers as placeholder or dummy text. Its purpose is to allow a focus on the visual layout and design of a document, typeface, or webpage without being distracted by meaningful content. Originating from sections of the Roman philosopher Cicero's work De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum (written in 45 BC), the text was first used in its scrambled form by an unknown printer in the 1500s to create type specimen books. Today, it remains the standard tool in digital and print media for demonstrating templates and prototypes before the final content is available.
